Hello

I am a Product Designer and a “Product Developer wanna be”.

I am based on Earth, you can find me everywhere and anywhere.

In creative world, my journey began in 2017 as a graphic designer where I used my skills in visual communication and aesthetics. However, winds blew me to the other side of the world. I started looking for other ways to create and solve problems at the same time.

A part from designing I have a bunch of other interests. But let’s start all from the very beginning.

I am Ana

Անահիտ

I was born and raised in a small town called Maartuni, Armeni in 1995. Growing up I had a bunch of questions about the big world. And because of asking non-stop questions to everyone I very soon got the nickname “ինչուիկ” (“inchuik” - little “why”).

My full name is Anahit (Անահիտ) given by my dad after a goddess of fertility and healing, wisdom and water.

My dad and auntie are professional chess players and growing up I played with figures instead of toys and learnt to plan my every step ahead. And my first job was a chess trainer at the age of 19. This is how I fell for teaching and my passion for creating and teaching grew stronger and bigger.
From 2013 to 2017 I studied in Yerevan State University majored in Philology (Language and Literature). I speak five languages, have two published books (horror novels and romantic poems) and a few articles about literature.
But in 2017 I did something crazy that changed my life forever. I took my little backpack, 200$ and ran away from home forever. I felt like my town was so small for a little girl with huge plans and dreams. And life took me to China.))

I got the nickname “cute Ana” from my students. They love teasing me and I love making a warm and comfortable environment for them to learn and love learning. I am passionate about cultivating a love for learning and ensuring that my students feel comfortable and supported in their educational journey.

Yes! I am also a teacher and the head of Art department in San-New Sino-American high school. If you wanna know more about my career and journey, simply check my resume or blogs.
